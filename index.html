Virtues, Sunnah & Resolution

How do I spice up my marriage?

Incorporate small acts of unexpected kindness, set aside intentional distraction-free quality time, and ensure consistent verbal affirmation to revive mutual intimacy.

Let's understand the virtue of marriage

In the Sharia context, marriage constitutes fulfilling half of one's religious path (Deen). It acts as a sacred fortress shielding chastity, cultivating emotional tranquility, and anchoring spiritual focus.

How to incorporate Sunnah into our marriage

Emulate the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) by maintaining a smiling disposition, actively assisting with everyday household responsibilities, and praying together as a unit to build localized spiritual blessings.

What are minor factors that drive mahabbah away?

Persistent minor criticisms, public corrections, neglect of basic physical hygiene, and ungrateful habits quietly corrode affection (Mahabbah) over long periods.

How do we resolve our marital problems?

Approach points of contention with genuine sincerity, isolate arguments immediately from external ears, and agree entirely to map out solutions strictly utilizing the parameters defined by Quranic directives and clean prophetic examples.
